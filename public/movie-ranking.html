The Best Movies of All-Time According to Me

My Top 3 Movies

  1. Scott Pilgrim vs. the World

    2. Very geeky, millennial-coded, but I still love it. To this day, I still put it on as background noise.

  2. The Shawshank Redemption

    3. I know, it's a cliche that every guy loves either Goodfellas, Fight Club, or this movie. It really is a great movie though.

  3. A Christmas Story

    4. I love a good coming-of-age story. Combine that with the wit of Jean Shepherd, who wrote the book the film is based on, In God We Trust, All Others Pay Cash, as well as narrates the film, and you have a perfect Christmas movie.
